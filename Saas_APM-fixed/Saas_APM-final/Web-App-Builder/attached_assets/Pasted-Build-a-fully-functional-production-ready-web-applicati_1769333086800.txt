Build a fully functional, production-ready web application called “PM-AI (Autonomous Product Manager AI)” designed specifically for SaaS products with subscription business models, where the system ingests real product usage events, UX interaction signals, and qualitative user feedback, normalizes and stores them in a persistent database, and uses an AI reasoning layer to generate explainable, revenue- and retention-weighted product and UX recommendations; the application must include a React-based frontend dashboard that displays SaaS product health metrics such as activation, retention, churn, expansion, trial-to-paid conversion, engagement frequency, and account health scores, alongside an AI recommendation feed where each recommendation is ranked by priority, estimated business impact, revenue influence, churn-reduction potential, and implementation effort, and where users can click into a detailed reasoning view showing the exact lifecycle stages affected (trial, activated, retained, churn-risk, churned), affected cohorts and plans (free, paid, SMB, enterprise), supporting analytics, and summarized user feedback evidence; implement a Node.js and Express backend that exposes REST APIs for ingesting SaaS events, feedback, feature metadata, and account information, performs lifecycle-aware preprocessing and aggregation of metrics across billing cycles, calculates SaaS-specific KPIs such as time-to-value, feature adoption by cohort, retention by plan, and churn risk signals, and persists all raw data and derived insights in a MongoDB database with clear schemas for users, accounts, events, feedback, features, plans, and recommendation history; create an AI intelligence layer that uses large language models for natural-language understanding of feedback, topic clustering, sentiment analysis, and decision reasoning, and combine these outputs with quantitative analytics in a deterministic SaaS-aware scoring engine that weights severity of pain, frequency, percentage of users affected, lifecycle sensitivity, account value, churn-reduction potential, expansion or upsell opportunity, and estimated engineering effort to compute a final impact score for every feature or UX flow; ensure the AI generates concrete, actionable recommendations such as onboarding flow improvements, feature redesigns, deprioritization decisions, retention interventions, and expansion-focused enhancements, and that every recommendation includes a concise natural-language explanation grounded in specific metrics, cohorts, and observed behaviors; implement a closed-loop learning mechanism where the system tracks whether recommendations are accepted, implemented, and their post-implementation impact on SaaS KPIs, and uses this outcome data to adjust future scoring confidence; the application should support historical comparison of recommendations over time, provide confidence indicators for AI outputs, and allow human review and approval before execution, with autonomy limited to analysis and recommendation generation rather than direct product changes; use React with Tailwind or Material UI for the frontend, Chart.js or Recharts for analytics visualizations, Node.js and Express for the backend, MongoDB with Mongoose for data modeling, and prompt-based AI pipelines integrated via OpenAI or HuggingFace models, and ensure the entire system runs end-to-end with real data ingestion, real AI reasoning, persistent storage, and a usable interface rather than mock data or placeholders, effectively simulating the analytical decision-making of an experienced SaaS product manager while remaining explainable, auditable, and aligned with business outcomes.
